Hi, I am Jenn Bhatti, A Researcher and UX Designer. My passion in psychology intersects with my artistic creativity which allows me to create and approach problems with a unique point of view.

My UX skills

User Research

My prior experience in clinical research has granted me with extensive experience in Data analysis as well as being able to collaboratively problem solve and develop solutions to problems that might arise while conducting a study.

User Empathy

In design it's no only important that the final product is conducive to a variety of users but also be able to do such requires a deeper understanding of the users needs and frustrations. Leading questions and allowing the user space to express themselves are good strategies to better understand the user.

Qualitative Interviews

When interviewing users it's important to have good quality questions that probe the user to further elaborate their responses. For example I will often ask users additional questions that hopefully will provide unique insight into possible overlooked issues.
